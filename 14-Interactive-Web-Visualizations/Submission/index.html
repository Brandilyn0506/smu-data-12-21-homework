Project summary

This study swabbed 60 belly buttons to see how much bacteria was found in them. Surprisingly enough, they found over 2300 speices of bacteria in these samples, with 8 species being the most prevalent. The Highest percentage was Corynebacterium, with it being found 31% of the time. This definitely may give you some pause, and make you scrub your belly button more! To see the bacteria that was found in each belly button, select a number from the dropdown. The graphs will update accordingly.
